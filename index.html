It's not just a myth!

Eating the right foods can lead to a longer, healthier life. To increase your lifespan, check out the major food groups that help you live longer. With the right contribution of food you can aim for a healthy balanced diet.

40%
Breads, pasta and cereals
At least 3 servings a day which the body uses for energy. Good choices are wholemeal, wholegrain bread, brown rice, couscous and polenta for carbohydrates.
35%
Fruit and vegetables
At least 3 servings of vegetables and 2 servings of fruits of different colours to provide a range of vitamins, minerals and fibre. One daily portion can be replaced with 2dl fruit juice with no additional sugar.
20%
Meat, fish, milk and eggs
2 to 3 servings a day with 1 serving of meat and at least 1 serving of milk or alternatives for protein, calcium, iron and zinc. Having enough calcium is necessary for healthy bones and teeth.
5%
Fat, oil, sugar, sweets
In very small amounts because they have only a little nutritional value. They should be consumed in moderation because they contain fat, sugar and/or salt.
0%
Alcohol and
other
Drink in moderation alcohol and sweet drinks or avoid it completely.
Instead drink water. It is recommended to drink at least 3 liters water per day.

Malnutrition shortens life

Nutrition is one of the important factors for longevity. Reasons for death and disease can be malnutrition such as overnutrition. Aside from nutrition, other factor determine the lifespan such as genetic, environmental and lifestyle. Life expectancy is held as an indicator for the social development of a country. With a healthy food balance you can increase the potential for a longer lifespan.
